Product Info

Frustrated with the available exercise equipment for a quadriplegic, the Wallgym was created.

  • Is a wall mounted unit covering just 3 feet in width that utilizes clip resistance bands that attach on different handles, bars, and attachments to perform a wide variety of exercises.
  • Can be attached to wall with lag bolts into two or more studs.


The Wallgym is designed for many types of people with varying requirements and conditions.
